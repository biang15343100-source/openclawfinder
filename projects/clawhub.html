What it is

ClawHub is part of the official tooling layer around OpenClaw. It serves as the place where skills can be found, installed, and shared.

Why it matters

A healthy ecosystem needs extension points, not only a runtime. ClawHub is one of the clearest signals that OpenClaw is trying to support a broader tool and skill marketplace.

Notable points

  • Documented in the official OpenClaw docs.
  • Backed by a public GitHub repository.
  • Good fit for a separate ecosystem category inside your directory.
