Official workflow format

OpenProse

The official workflow format for chaining agents, tools, and steps into more structured OpenClaw flows.

Description

OpenProse is the official workflow layer documented around OpenClaw. It is positioned as the way to express more structured orchestration instead of running everything through one flat assistant loop.

Its importance is strategic more than flashy. Mature ecosystems need workflow abstraction, not just tools and agents. OpenProse signals that OpenClaw is already thinking about programmable composition and repeatable multi-agent flows.

  • Officially documented under the OpenClaw docs site.
  • Useful as a separate ecosystem entry rather than as a runtime replacement.
  • A strong base for future examples around comparisons and workflow templates.
