Security-first Rust branch

IronClaw

A Rust branch centered on privacy, validation, and safer execution for teams that care about trust boundaries.

Description

IronClaw is the branch to watch if your first concern is not convenience but trust boundaries. Public materials frame it as a Rust implementation with dedicated safety layers, stronger validation, and a clearer privacy-oriented execution model.

Compared with the original project, it is less about giving you the broadest ecosystem entry and more about making the runtime feel safer, more auditable, and easier to reason about in sensitive environments.

  • Rust and safety-focused architecture are central to its public positioning.
  • A better fit for security-minded teams than for first-time casual users.
  • Best understood as a hardened branch, not the easiest front door into the ecosystem.
